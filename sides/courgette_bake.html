Antonia's Courgette Bake

  • 900g courgettes, peeled and cut into rounds
  • 450g tomatoes, skinned and chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Soft breadcrumbs

Put courgettes into colander and sprinkle with salt. Leave for 30 mins then drain and blot dry. Fry courgettes in butter, then transfer to dish. Fry tomatoes, garlic, parsley in butter with salt and pepper until liquid has reduced. Amalgamate with courgettes in dish, sprinkle with layer of breadcrumbs and dot with butter. Bake at 220 degrees for 25-30 mins.
