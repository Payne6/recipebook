Keira's Marmite Roast Potatoes

  • Medium size floury potatoes
  • Marmite

Preheat oven to 180 degrees. Peel potatoes and cut into large chunks. Put in pan, cover with water and bring to the boil. Simmer for 12 mins. Meanwhile, mix marmite and fat/olive oil in small bowl, then empty into roasting tin and put in oven to heat for 10 mins. Drain potatoes, take hot fat mixture out of oven, add potatoes and seasoning and turn to coat. Roast for 1 hour, turning occasionally, until golden.
