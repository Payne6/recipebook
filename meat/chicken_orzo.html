Chicken with Orzo

  • 4 chicken breasts, chopped into large pieces
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 100ml red wine
  • 1 litre chicken stock
  • 500ml passata
  • 7cm cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 300g orzo pasta
  • Parmesan cheese
Brown the chicken pieces in a frying pan. Add onion, garlic and red pepper and cook for approx 5 mins. Add red wine, chicken stock, passata, cinnamon stick, oregano, salt and pepper. Bring to the boil then reduce heat and cover pan with a lid. Simmer for 20-25 mins. Add pasta to sauce, replace lid on pan, simmer for a further 10-15 mins. Serve with grated parmesan.
