Borlotti Bean Soup

  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped into small cubes
  • 200g bacon, chopped
  • 3-4 pork belly slices, chopped into large chunks
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 1.3 litres chicken stock
  • 1/3 packet kale
  • 1 tin borlotti beans
  • Small handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped

Fry onion and carrots in large pan for 5 mins, then add bacon and pork belly. Allow to cook for 5 mins, then add paprika, oregano, chili flakes and tomato puree and stir well. Add chicken stock, bring to boil, then add pepper. Turn heat down to a simmer. Put lid on pan, slightly ajar and cook for 1.5-2 hours. Add beans and kale, leave to cook for additional ten mins then season and serve with parsley.
