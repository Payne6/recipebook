Steve's White Pasta

  • 150g lardons
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 6-7 mushrooms
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 small courgette
  • Greek yoghurt
  • 200ml creme fraiche
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 300g pasta

Fry the lardons in butter and olive oil. Add the onion, then the garlic and chili. Fry on medium heat until the onion softens, then add chicken. Cook until chicken is brown, then add mushrooms and courgettes and cook gently for 10-15 mins. Lower heat and add a cupful of Greek yoghurt and stir well. Add herbs, eg., parsley, tarragon, basil, then turn off heat and transfer to a small/medium size saucepan and leave for 1 hour. Boil the pasta. Meanwhile, gently reheat the sauce, incrementally adding creme fraiche. When pasta is cooked, drain, add knob of butter, season well, then add sauce to pasta. Serve with parmesan cheese.
