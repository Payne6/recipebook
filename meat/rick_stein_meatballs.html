Rick Stein's Meatballs

  • 500g minced lamb or beef
  • 150g breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Handful mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • Olive oil
  • 30ml white wine
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Blend together the breadcrumbs, mint, oregano, onion, garlic, oil and white wine. In a large bowl mix with meat and egg and add salt and pepper. Roll into golf-ball sized meatballs. Fry in olive oil over a medium heat.
