Bacon and Pea Pasta

  • 10 slices bacon
  • Small bunch fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 150g parmasan cheese
  • 400g pasta
  • 1 knob butter
  • 300g frozen peas
  • 3 large spoonfuls creme fraiche
  • 1 lemon

Boil pasta. While pasta is cooking, chop and fry bacon in pan with olive oil and butter. Sprinkle black pepper over bacon and grate cheese. When bacon is crisp add peas to pan. After 1 minute add creme fraiche and mint. Drain pasta (reserving some of the cooking water) and add to pan. Halve lemon and squeeze over pasta. when sauce is bubbling (add reserved water if too thick) remove pan from heat, add cheese and serve.
