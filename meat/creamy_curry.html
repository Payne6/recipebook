Creamy Chicken Curry

  • 1.35kg chicken breast
  • 2 tsp ground cumin seeds
  • 2.5 tsp ground coriander seeds
  • 1.5 tsp ground turmeric
  • 0.5 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 inch cube ginger, peeled and coarsely chopped
  • 110g onion
  • 175g chopped tomato
  • 4 tbsp plain yoghurt
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 6 tbsp double cream

Sprinkle half spice mixture (cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne) and some black pepper over chicken then set chicken aside for 1 hour. Blend garlic and ginger with 125ml water until fairly smooth. Brown chicken in large pot over medium heat then set aside. Add onion to pan, when brown add garlic and ginger paste and fry until water from paste evaporates. Add rest of spice mixture. Add chopped tomato and lower heat. Stir of 3-4 mins then add yoghurt 1 tbsp at a time. Add chicken with 300ml water and 1 tsp salt. Bring to the boil, cover, the reduce heat and simmer for 20mins. Add garam masala and cream and serve.
