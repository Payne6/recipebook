Spaghetti Carbonara

  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 large knob butter
  • 250g bacon lardons
  • Three egg yolks plus one whole egg, beaten
  • Two tablespoons parmesan
  • Spaghetti

Boil the spaghetti. Meanwhile, fry the garlic and the lardons in the butter. When spagetti has boiled, lift it onto the lardons and stir in the eggs and cheese, adding some of the starchy pasta water until the sauce is creamy. Season and serve.
