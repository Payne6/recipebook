Goulash

  • 1 kg braising steak
  • Sunflower oil
  • 3 medium onions cut into 12 wedges
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 4 tblspoon hot paprika
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 600ml cold water
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbspoon tomato puree
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 peppers (1 red, 1 orange, 1 green)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cut meat into chunks and season with salt and pepper. Heat oil in large casserole dish, add steak and fry until browned. Add onions and cook for 5 mins until softened. Add garlic and cook for 1 min then sprinkle paprika and crush stock cube over meat. Add water, tomatoes, tomato puree and bay leaves, season, stir and bring to a simmer. Cover with lid then transfer to oven and cook for 90 mins at 170 degrees. Chop up peppers, add to dish then cook in oven for a further hour. Serve with rice.
