Impostor Pasta

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 300g cherry tomatoes
  • 2 x 150g Boursin cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 250g pasta
  • Dried mixed herbs

Place cheese in centre of baking tray with tomatoes, garlic and chicken. Sprinkle with herbs and season then bake for 25 mins at 200 degrees.
