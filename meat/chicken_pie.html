Chicken Pie

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 knob butter
  • 5-6 spring onions
  • 1 leek
  • 1/2 box mushrooms
  • 1 large dollop creme fraiche
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 1 tsp english mustard
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • Puff pastry
  • 1 egg

Fry chicken in pan with salt, pepper and butter. Chop and add spring onions, leek and mushrooms. Add creme fraiche, flour and mustard and transfer to a casserole dish. Add stock then cover dish with puff pastry, tucking in the edges. Beat the egg then spread over the pastry. Place in oven and cook for 15 mins at 200 degrees.
