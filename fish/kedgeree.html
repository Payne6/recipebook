Kedgeree

  • Cooked rice (enough for two)
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • Curry powder
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • Cooked/tinned fish, roughly chopped
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 knob butter
  • Handful chives, chopped

Fry onions in pan until soft and brown. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 2 more mins, then dd curry powder. Mix well, then add rice and mix again. Add butter and tumble rice and butter together in pan. Chop eggs into quarters and add to mixture. Sprinkle chives over top and serve.
