Fish and Asparagus

  • 2 pieces smoked salmon
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 lemon
  • Spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp creme fraiche

Add spaghetti to pan of boiling water. Meanwhile add fish to another pan and fry on medium heat. While fish is frying, peel asparagus with a potato peeler until it becomes difficult then finely chop the rest. Add chopped asparagus to pan. Sprinkle with lemon zest. Cook for 1 min then squeeze lemon juice into pan. Add cooked spaghetti to pan then add creme fraiche and mix together. Add water from spaghetti pan if too thick. Add the asparagus ribbons then serve.
