Red Tuna

  • 1 red chili
  • 4 anchovies
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 courgette
  • Handful black olives
  • 1/4 jar capers
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin tuna
  • Rice

Put rice on to boil. Meanwhile, chop chili, garlic and anchovies and fry in oil for 1 min. Chop courgette, add to pan and stir. When courgette is beginning to brown, chop olives and add to pan with capers. Fry for 1 min then add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 mins. Add tuna. Serve with rice.
