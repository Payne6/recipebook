Tom and Keira's Fish Pie

  • 5 large potatoes
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 250ml double cream
  • Cheddar or parmasan
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp english mustard
  • 1 handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • 450g haddock or cod

Peel and dice potatoes into 2.5cm chunks then boil for 2 mins. Add eggs and cook for 8 mins. Meanwhile steam spinach into colander over pan (approx 1 min) then remove from colander and squeeze out any excess moisture. Drain potatoes in colander and peel and quarter eggs. Finely chop onion and carrot, fry in separate pan for 5 mins, then add double cream and bring to the boil. Remove from heat, grate in two handfuls of cheese then squeeze in lemon juice and add mustard and parsley. Slice fish into strips, place in earthenware dish with spinach and eggs, mix together then add sauce. Drain and mash potatoes (add sea salt and black pepper) then spread on top of fish. Cook for 30 mins at 230 degrees until golden.
