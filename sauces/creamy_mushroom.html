Creamy Mushroom Sauce

  • Olive oil
  • 1 knob butter
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 glass white wine
  • 1/2 glass chicken stock
  • 1 pot cream
  • Handful parmasan cheese, grated
  • Thyme

Fry mushrooms in olive oil and butter. Add garlic and stir together. Add wine then stock then cream. Add cheese and thyme and cook until thickened.
